# **Sprint 1: Execution**

### **Goal**

The primary objective of this sprint is to deliver your **first increment of working software** and establish your **DevOps pipeline**.

### ---

**Key Requirements**

* **Deliver Work:** You must complete and deliver at least **two** of your planned backlog items from Sprint 0\.

* **Version Control (Git):** \* You must use Git for all work.

  * Your commit history must show **iterative, incremental delivery**. "Big-bang" commits (uploading everything at the end) are not allowed.

* **Set up CI/CD:** Implement a basic **CI pipeline** using tools like GitHub Actions or GitLab CI.

* **Implement Testing:** Write and integrate **unit or integration tests** that run automatically within your CI pipeline.

* **Sprint Review:** Produce a document or short demo (such as screenshots or a write-up) that showcases exactly what you delivered.

* **Retrospective:** Write a reflection identifying at least **two specific process improvements** to implement in Sprint 2\.
